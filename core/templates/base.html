What is “Swarna Sampadha”?

The purpose of this document is to provide a detailed specification for the development of an DIAMONDS ecommerce website. An DIAMOND ecommerce site is an online platform where customers can browse, compare, and purchase DIAMOND products such as Nose Pin, Ear Rings, Rings, Necklace, and more. These sites are designed to provide a virtual shopping experience for DIAMONDS, allowing users to explore a wide range of products, read reviews, and make purchases without having to visit a physical store.
